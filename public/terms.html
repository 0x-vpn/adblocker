Using the service

0x VPN provides access to VPN connectivity through supported Android builds. You are responsible for using the service lawfully and for complying with rules that apply in your country, network, Android device policy, and VPN location.

Accounts and recovery keys

Signup creates a username-based account and returns a one-time recovery key. Keep the recovery key private and stored somewhere safe. Anyone with the recovery key may be able to access the account.

VPN minutes and rewards

VPN access may depend on available VPN minutes. Rewarded ads are optional and grant minutes only after server-side verification. Rewards can be refused, delayed, or reversed if verification fails, a duplicate reward is detected, or abuse controls are triggered.

VPN behavior

Android shows a system VPN permission prompt before connection. While connected, device or selected-app traffic is routed through the chosen VPN tunnel according to the mode selected in the app.

Ad blocking limits

Optional DNS ad blocking is best-effort and local to tunneled apps. It does not decrypt HTTPS, install a root certificate, or guarantee that all ads are blocked. Some apps, encrypted DNS, first-party ads, and first-party media delivery may bypass DNS filtering.

Acceptable use

Do not use 0x VPN to attack networks, bypass lawful restrictions in a way that violates applicable law, send spam, distribute malware, scrape or abuse services, create repeated abusive accounts, or interfere with other users or infrastructure.

Updates and availability

The app may check a public HTTPS manifest for updates. Direct APK updates still require Android user approval. Service locations, VPN minutes, rewards, and backend functions may change, be limited, or be unavailable during maintenance or abuse-prevention events.
